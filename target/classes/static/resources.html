🧮 Gardening Formulas & Examples

1️⃣ Garden Area:
Area = Length × Width
Example: If your garden is 6m long and 4m wide → Area = 24 m²
2️⃣ Number of Plants:
Plants = Area ÷ Plant Spacing
Example: 24 m² ÷ 0.5m spacing → 48 plants
3️⃣ Water Usage per Week:
Total Water = Plants × Litres per day × 7
Example: 48 plants × 0.5 L × 7 = 168 L/week
4️⃣ Cost Estimation:
Total Cost = (Seeds + Fertilizer + Water + Tools)
5️⃣ Yield Prediction:
Yield = Plants × Average yield per plant
